About Snap Shot 2017

This year we will be hosting the first Snap Shot conference. Whether your thinking of getting in to photography or just want to sharpen your skills, Snap Shot has it all.

Snap Shot 2017 will be a one day event consisting of presentations, hands-on workshops as well as lots of networking opporunities. Speakers and attendees will travel from all over to attend this one day event, held in Swansea, South Wales (UK).
