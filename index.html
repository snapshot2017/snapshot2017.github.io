About Snap Shot 2017

July 2017 will be the first Take conference. Whether your thinking of getting in to photography or just want to sharpen your skills, Take has it all.

Take is a one day event consisting of presentations, hands-on workshops as well as lots of networking opporunities. Speakers and attendees will travel from all over the world to attend this one day event, held in Swansea, South Wales (UK).
